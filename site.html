Welcome to a Brief Guide to Swimming

WELCOME to swimming! If you don't know already, swimming is one of the few types of exercise done in the water. It utilizes every aspect of your body, your legs, arms, and even your brain.

You've probably heard of swimming when people try to improve their heart health, well it's true! Swimming is great for your heart, physically and metaphorically!
